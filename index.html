Our Mission

TechNova empowers young women and allies to explore STEM, build skills, and connect with a supportive community. Through workshops, mentorship, and hands-on projects, we aim to inspire curiosity, confidence, and innovation in the next generation of tech leaders.

What We Do

From coding workshops and robotics projects to guest speaker panels and mentorship opportunities, **TechNova** provides hands-on experiences that spark curiosity and build real skills. Members collaborate, learn, and grow together while exploring a wide range of STEM fields.

About Us

Founded in 2023, **TechNova** began with a small group of high school students passionate about closing the gender gap in STEM fields. We recognized the need for a supportive, inclusive, and challenging environment where future female leaders could thrive.

Today, our organization is run by a dedicated team of student volunteers and supported by professional mentors from the tech industry. We are committed to fostering a community where every member feels empowered to innovate, build, and connect with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join TechNova?

Our club is primarily designed for high school students interested in STEM, but we welcome all genders, including male allies, who support our mission of promoting diversity in technology.

Is there a fee to join the club?

No, there are no fees or dues required to become a member of TechNova. Some events, like competition travel, may have voluntary contributions, but core activities are free.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Absolutely not! Most of our workshops are designed for beginners. We aim to make STEM accessible, so if you are curious, we can teach you the rest!

How often does the club meet?

We typically hold weekly workshops and bi-weekly speaker sessions or networking meetups. Check the "Upcoming Events" section for the latest schedule.
