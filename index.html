About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the TechNova experience.

How do workshops work?

We host specialized workshops 1-2 times per month. These are intensive mini-courses on advanced topics or unique networking sessions with local industry leaders and guest speakers.

Are the classes free?

Yes. All classes, workshops, and resources are completely free. We believe in providing equal tech opportunities for all youth, regardless of financial background.

Java or AI Optimization?

Choice is encouraged! We offer separate curriculums for both. Students can choose the path that best suits their goals! Keep in mind students must be at least 14 to begin our AI Track.

Why Java?

Java powers billions of devices and is a core language for AP Computer Science. It provides a rock-solid foundation for future software engineering careers.

Can complete beginners join?

Absolutely. TechNova is built to welcome absolute beginners. Our low-pressure environment is designed to turn curiosity into confidence.

Where do I sign up?

Simply click the "Join TechNova" button at the top of the page.

Confirmation via email within 1-2 business days.

Weekly Classes

Register for Classes →

We offer weekly classes for all ages, designed to fit your child's learning style. We offer two different curriculums: Java Basics, which teaches the fundamentals of text-based, object-oriented programming, and Block Coding Fundamentals, which provides a visual, drag-and-drop introduction to logic and problem-solving.

Monthly Workshops

We typically host 1-2 workshops every month. These events can range anywhere from 2 to 5 days, depending on the topic. Workshops often include guest speakers from the industry, opportunities to learn new developmental skills, and other exciting opportunities, so stay on the lookout!

Peer Mentorship

Request a Mentor →

Need help with a tricky school assignment or want expert eyes on a personal project? Our peer mentorship program is here for you. We offer free, one-on-one help with a variety of subjects. Whether it's Coding, Math, English or guidance on your own passion project, our experienced mentors are ready to help you succeed.
